Our Mission

Any foodie worth their weight in salt should always have ideas of where to eat in their home city whenever the opportunity arises (date night, a rare free night, girls night, bachelor party, etc.). If you are tired of defaulting to the same dive spot because of "decision fatigue" every week, Restaurant Passport is the solution. View the restaurants you've been to, have suggestions at your fingertips for any occasion and taste, and brag to your friends about all the cool eateries you've been to!
